Crazy Caves

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 5.0 (15 Votes)

Developer: Famobi

Technology: HTML5

Platform: Browser

High Demand Motor Skills 2D Drag to move Obstacle Avoid

Crazy Caves is an exhilarating 2D arcade-style web game that puts you in control of a determined miner in a perilous underground adventure. Your goal is simple: survive as long as possible while collecting valuable gold coins.

Drag your miner left and right to dodge falling rocks, while simultaneously aiming and throwing pickaxes to destroy them. Each destroyed rock leaves behind gold coins for you to collect. As you progress, the challenge intensifies with more rocks and faster gameplay.

With its addictive gameplay and endless challenge, Crazy Caves offers colorful 2D graphics and intuitive controls that make it easy to start but challenging to master. How deep can you dig before the caves get too crazy?
